Addition.

Written by Big Brother on December 14, 1984.
 Addition is a mathematical operation that represents the total amount of objects together in a collection. It is signified by the plus sign (+). 
 For example, in the picture on the right, there are 3 + 2 apples—meaning three apples and two apples together, which is a total of 5 apples. Therefore, 3 + 2 = 5. 
 Besides counting fruits, addition can also represent combining other physical and abstract quantities using different kinds of 
     objects: negative numbers, fractions, irrational numbers, vectors, decimals, functions, matrices and more.
